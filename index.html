Making the Sport of Kings the Sport of Students

DUPC is a nationally and internationally competing sports team and university community, one wholly dedicated to making the majestic and exilerating sport of polo as assessible and diverse as possible.

"A polo handicap is a persons ticket to the world."

Winston Churchill

Training at the Best Clubs in Northen England



We may train about an hour and half away, but the quality of teaching makes it worth the time. Uppers and beginners train in different polo clubs, suited to their abilities.

Uppers: Training at Bramham Polo Club, Fran takes us on an exilerating ride in fast pace chukkas suited for returners.

Beginners:Training at White Rose, no one is better than Will at introducing beginners to the game. He will perfect your technique and go over tactics until you could recite them in your sleep.
